About the PNGHS Choir

Founded in 1965, the The Palmerston North Girls High School (PNGHS) Choir seeks to enhance the musical development of children by instilling in them a love of ensemble singing. By striving for artistic excellence and learning a diverse repertory of choral literature, our children build self-confidence and learn the joys of working together to create beautiful music for an appreciative audience.

Artistic Director

Born and raised in Eastern Manawatu, David Chen studied conducting, voice, and piano at Massey University at Palmerston North. David returned to Palmerston North two years ago to take over direction of the PNGS Choir. The children appreciate David's demand for quality performances while still maintaining a fun and playful atmosphere.

As a performer, he has sung in Auckland with the Auckland Choral Artists under the direction of Helen Von Allen. He has also been working on a project to edit and perform the sacred works of Hildegard of Bingen. Look for the premier of his efforts next summer.
